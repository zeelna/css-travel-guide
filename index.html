Visit Saaremaa

Enjoy culture and countryside in the largest island of Kuressaare in Estonia.

Top three activities to do at Saaremaa

Explore the Museum of ANGLA Windmills

Saaremaa preserves heritage sites of historical windmills that fed the entire island. Reenact the intricate work to grain wheat with Windmills .

Visit the Old Town near Castle of KURESSAARE

Kuressaare has a medieval Old Town with many restaurants, scenic routes and The Castle of Kuressaare.

Look upon the coastline atop the lighthouses

The coastline of Saaremaa is riddled with Lighthouses open to visitors for any to reach the top.
